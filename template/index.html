Merge PDF FAQs.

  1. In what order will my merged PDF files apper?

    +

    Once you add the files you'd like to combine, you can drag and drop them into your preferred order. The top file in your list will appear first in the merged PDF file.

  2. How many pages can I include in a merged PDF?

    +

    The Acrobat Merge PDFs tool lets you create a merged, single PDF document of up to 1,500 pages. You can combine up to 100 files, with each individual file limited to 500 pages.

  3. Can I delete and reorder pages after I merge files?

    +

    When you combine PDF files, you can reorder, add, or delete files before you merge them into a single document. Sign in if you need to reorganize individual pages in your merged PDF. You can add, delete, move, or rotate PDF pages as needed until your content is in the desired order.
